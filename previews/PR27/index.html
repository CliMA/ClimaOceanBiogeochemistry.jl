ClimaOceanBiogeochemistry

ClimaOceanBiogeochemistry.jl provides biogeochemistry models that integrate with Oceananigans.jl and ClimaOcean.jl.

See also the Oceananigans documentation.

ClimaOceanBiogeochemistry.jl is developed by the Climate Modeling Alliance and heroic external collaborators.

Installation

To add ClimaOceanBiogeochemistry.jl to your local environment, type

julia> using Pkg

julia> Pkg.add("https://github.com/CliMA/ClimaOceanBiogeochemistry.jl.git")
